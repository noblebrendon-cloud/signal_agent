# AI Stability Snapshot - 10 Questions

## Instructions
Answer each question Yes (1) or No (0). Total your score at the end.

## Questions
1. Are your workflows documented end-to-end?
2. Does one person hold critical process knowledge?
3. Are AI outputs reviewed by a defined owner?
4. Do you have version control on shared documents?
5. Can you map where your operational data lives?
6. Are multiple AI tools solving overlapping problems?
7. Do you track automation errors or failures?
8. Is there a rollback plan if automation fails?
9. Has AI reduced workload or only shifted it?
10. If usage doubled tomorrow, what would break first?

### Part 2: Epistemic & Causal Stability
11. Do you externalize causal interpretation, or only telemetry?
12. Can two teams produce the same root cause story from the same evidence?
13. Is there a cap on concurrent hypotheses during incidents?
14. Are mechanisms and counterfactuals enforced as required fields before taking action?
15. Do postmortems update a living causal knowledge base rather than gathering dust?
## Score
- Yes = 1
- No = 0

## Interpretation
- 7-10: Stable foundation
- 4-6: Moderate risk
- 0-3: High instability risk
